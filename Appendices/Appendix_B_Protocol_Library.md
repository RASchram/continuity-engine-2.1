## 7. Example Protocol (Fully Written)

### **Protocol: 90‑Second Grounding Protocol**

**Trigger:**  
Emotional load spikes, clarity drops, or drift appears.

**Purpose:**  
Stabilize emotional fidelity and restore cognitive clarity.

**Steps:**  
1. Stop movement and breathe once deeply.  
2. Place attention on the body’s center of gravity.  
3. Identify the emotional signature without naming it.  
4. Breathe slowly for 60 seconds.  
5. Re‑scan emotional fidelity.  
6. Capture a micro‑scroll if needed.  
7. Resume action with restored clarity.

**Notes:**  
This protocol prevents emotional artifacts from entering structural reasoning.
